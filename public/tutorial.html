Tutorial Create



Welcome to Seul.





1. How to Create?
You can Create Photo Frame!
Go to Create!
2. How to Join?
You can Join Photo Frame!
Click the Join Frame!

If the frame is already full
or you have previously joined,
you can't Join!
Go to Join!
3. How to take a picture?
Step:
Click the "Choose File" button
and select "Camera" to take a picture!
Recommendation:
We suggest using the Auto rotate feature for horizontal pictures!
4. How to continue using Seul.
iOS:
Open Seul. in Safari, tap share,
select "Add to Home Screen."
Android:
Visit Seul. in Chrome, tap the three dots,
choose "Install App" or "Add to Home Screen."

Now, access Seul. directly from your home screen anytime.
